Whose Democracy

Just as LinkedIn serves as a platform for keeping up with your professional network, Whose Democracy is a full-stack web app for keeping up with your civic network. It will provide users the ability to easily draft, save, and send emails to Senate and House members, and view, like, and comment on letters that friends have sent to their own representatives. The goal is to make contacting representatives a socially collaborative activity and to provide a social platform that encourages everyday civic engagement.

Whose Democracy uses a Postgres database, Ruby on Rails on the backend, and JavaScript with React/Redux on the frontend. To make emailing representatives easy and user-friendly, it will use SmartyStreets Geocoding API to look up users' representatives based on their street address, the ProPublica Congress API to display the most up-to-date voting records of congress members, and the Phantom of the Capitol API to fill out the contact forms of members of the US Congress without having to navigate away from the app.
